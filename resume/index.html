Leo Bonet is a multimedia producer with interdisciplinary experience in Graphic Design, 3D, Motion Graphics & Animation, Music Production, Creative Writing, and Web.

He was born in Venezuela and has been a resident of Orlando, FL since 2015. He has a Bachelor of Arts in Architecture, an Associate of Arts in Audio Engineering & Music Production, an Associate in Science in Digital Media, and technical certificates in Graphic Design Content Development, Animation and Visual Effects, and Digital and Interactive Media Design. He also speaks English, Spanish, Italian, and French.

Leo and his wife Nina write and produce content for their project The Glam Bang, a musical band that exists inside a graphic novel. “A Cosmic Book” will be their first book-album to be released.
